About

Dallas: New Horizon

Deep in the heart of Texas, the city of Dallas was where my journey began, not as the web developer and analyst you see today, but as a fervent graduate student. The vibrant Dallas skyline wasn't the only thing reaching for the stars; I too was on my own quest for knowledge and expertise. Surrounded by the city's dynamic blend of cultures, I embarked on my academic journey with the same fervor and enthusiasm. It was here that I transitioned from a mere student to a budding developer. The challenges were many, but every line of code I wrote and every analysis I conducted was a step closer to my dreams.

Naples: Kiss the Sun

Just as I was getting comfortable with the Dallas skyline, destiny had other plans. With a diploma in one hand and a suitcase in the other, I found myself in Naples, Florida. The sun-kissed beaches and the gentle sway of the palm trees were a stark contrast to the bustling streets of Dallas. I joined a pioneering telemedicine company, where my skills as a web developer and analyst played a crucial role in bridging the gap between patients and healthcare professionals. In the face of adversity, I learned the true meaning of resilience and adaptability.
